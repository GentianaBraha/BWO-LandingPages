Weft
Extensions

Weft extensions are a salon favorite application method, with no glue, no heat and no damage. Lasting 4 to 6 weeks before re-application is needed, the Micro Ring Weft or Braided Weft Hair Extensions applications are a great form of semi permanent hair extensions, adding volume, length and colour to natural hair. Beauty Works offer salon quality weft-in hair extensions that are totally reusable for multiple application.
